Financial Decision Making

The system uses probabilistic density functions and simulation to analyze demand behavior. It helps SMEs in the dairy sector make informed financial decisions by providing insights into demand patterns and forecasts.

Probabilistic density functions are mathematical tools used to model and describe the probability distribution of random variables. In our system, they are applied to analyze and predict demand variations in the dairy sector.
